About Us

Logic Development and Design (LDD) started in early 2001 with providing software solutions for businesses in the Calgary area and ensuring their transition into the new tech age was seamless and scalable as new technology advanced.

 

LDD has been committed to delivering advanced software solutions to ensure your company remains on the cutting edge. Over 1000 projects have been completed for clients of all size, across many fields including financial services, government projects, energy sector clients and small businesses.

 

In 2012 LDD expanded its offering from the Calgary area to cover all of western Canada and as been committed to providing the same level of service to its customers across the broader western Canada area.

 

Going forward. LDD will be expanding its reach into eastern Canada at the start of 2019 and will be ensuring that the standards we have built up are maintained and our products are delivered to the clients exact needs
